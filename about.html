OUR STORY

Zebra was built to end a common hassle that all drivers share, filling up one’s car. Here at Zebra, we plan on disrupting the fuel industry by making the act of filling up your car a moment of joy instead of agony.

Using our proprietary vehicles, we dispense fuel to all our customers in a safe, scheduled, and professional manner. All you have to do is download our app, locate your car, select the quantity of fuel you require, and we take care of the rest.
