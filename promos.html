HOW DOES THE £20 OFF PROMOTION WORK?

Here at Zebra Fuel, we love discounts and we love our customers - that’s why we’re giving all of our customers £20 off their first order with us!

We believe that once you try Zebra Fuel, you will never need to visit another petrol station again! This offer only applies to deliveries made in the following postcodes in London: SW7, SW3, SW5, W14, SW10, SW6.

Once you order for the first time on Zebra, your credit card will be charged for the full amount you ordered but you will also be issued an instant refund for the amount you ordered (if it totaled less than £20) or £20, whichever is less. In other words, if you placed an order for less than £20, then the full amount of your order will be refunded. £20 is the maximum refundable amount.

You will be sent an automated email confirming that the refund has been issued by our payment provider. Please note that the refund can take up to 7 days to show on your personal bank statement.
