Logistics Expert

We are looking for a logistics expert who wants to disrupt a traditional business model and help us revolutionize the way people interact with fuel. Our vision is a world without gas stations where your phone replaces the need to go fill-up your car and get your hands dirty. We need you to oversee and improve our overall operational system, which will include streamlining our fuel supply chain and at least initially, managing our fleet of drivers. We need someone who is not afraid of growing operational issues and is confident in their ability to optimize logistical issues. You will be working at the highest level of Zebra and will be given serious equity. We are looking for someone with strong leadership and confidence.

Job Requirements:

  • Live/be based in London
  • Qualified to work in the UK
  • 5+ years working directly in operations/logistics, preferably involving the fuel industry
  • Minimum bachelor qualification
  • See the big picture

Why join:

  • Serious equity
  • Financial compensation depending on experience
  • Full benefits
  • Chance to be part of something truly disruptive

Zebra Driver

We are looking for drivers who have held a valid UK Driving license for more than 5 years based in London, UK. Your role as a Zebra Driver will be to help us eliminate the need for our customers to ever use a gas station again. We pride ourselves on our service and our team will take you through all the required procedures to ensure you are compliant with regulations applicable to our deliveries. We will provide you with the appropriate training (i.e. hazmat certification, ADR) and will reward hard work and dedication. As a member of #TeamZebra you will also be offered equity in the business as well as bonuses and benefits. We treat our employees like family.

Job Requirements:

  • UK/EU citizen able to work in the UK
  • UK driver’s license with 5+ years of driving time
  • Experience working with deliveries/customer interactions
  • Job will be Mon-Fri (8:30am – 5pm)
  • Optional paid weekends

Why join:

  • Company equity
  • Zebra uniform supplied
  • Regular work schedule
  • Full benefits
  • Competitive salary
  • Full training provided by Zebra
